InfiniQue At A Glance

Utilizing Device-to-Device Technologies

With the developement of mesh networks, InfiniQue relies on peer-to-peer technology allowing automated and fast connectivity across devices instantenously. Share your music taste with people around you without having to connect to internet.

Simultaneous connectivity for multiple users to same speaker, meaning only one person needs to be connected to the speaker and content is derived from a network of devices around you. Playlists are synchronised across devices on off-line mesh network, allowing devices to contribute content.
